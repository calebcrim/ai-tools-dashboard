## Content Creation Workflow

**Step 1: Topic Research (10 min)**
Prompt: "What are the top 5 trending topics in [industry] this month?"

**Step 2: Keyword Research (5 min)**
Prompt: "Generate 10 SEO keywords for [chosen topic]"

**Step 3: Outline Creation (10 min)**
Prompt: "Create a detailed outline for a 2000-word article on [topic] targeting [primary keyword]"

**Step 4: Content Generation (20 min)**
Prompt: "Write section [X] of the article following this outline: [paste outline]"

**Step 5: SEO Enhancement (10 min)**
Prompt: "Optimize this content for the keyword '[keyword]' without keyword stuffing"

**Step 6: Final Polish (15 min)**
- Add visuals
- Internal links
- Fact-check
- Brand voice check
