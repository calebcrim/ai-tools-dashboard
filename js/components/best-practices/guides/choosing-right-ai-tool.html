Our proven 5-step framework for AI tool selection:

M

Mission Define

What specific problem are you trying to solve?

  • Write down your exact use case
  • List must-have vs nice-to-have features
  • Define success metrics
A

Assess Options

Research tools that match your mission

  • Category-specific tools vs general purpose
  • Read real user reviews (not just marketing)
  • Check integration capabilities
T

Always try before you buy

  • Use free trials for real work
  • Test with your actual use cases
  • Measure against success metrics
