Advanced Techniques üöÄ

1. Negative Examples

Show what NOT to do for even better results

Good example: "Our eco-friendly bottles keep you hydrated sustainably" Bad example (avoid this): "Buy our bottles! They're the best! Amazing quality!" Now write product descriptions avoiding promotional language.

2. Progressive Complexity

Start simple, then add complexity

Simple: "The blue shirt - $29" Medium: "Classic Blue Oxford - Timeless style meets modern comfort. $29" Complex: "The Heritage Oxford in Ocean Blue - Crafted from organic cotton with mother-of-pearl buttons. A versatile essential that transitions effortlessly from boardroom to weekend. $29" Now create descriptions for our premium line.

3. Edge Case Examples

Include tricky scenarios in your examples

Standard: Process order for in-stock item Edge case 1: Process order when item is backlogged Edge case 2: Process order with multiple shipping addresses Show examples covering these scenarios...
