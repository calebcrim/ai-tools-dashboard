Advanced Role Techniques üöÄ

1. Multi-Role Perspectives

Get diverse viewpoints by asking AI to consider multiple roles:

Analyze this business idea from three perspectives: 1. As a venture capitalist focused on ROI 2. As a customer experience designer 3. As a risk management consultant Provide insights from each role.

2. Role + Personality Traits

Combine expertise with communication style:

Act as a data scientist who is also an excellent storyteller. You make complex statistics accessible through vivid analogies and real-world examples.

3. Adversarial Roles

Use opposing roles to stress-test ideas:

First, act as an enthusiastic startup founder pitching this idea. Then, act as a skeptical investor pointing out every possible flaw. Finally, synthesize both perspectives.

üåü Best Practices

  • Be Specific: "Senior DevOps Engineer" beats "IT Professional"
  • Add Context: Include industry, company size, or specific challenges
  • Set Expectations: Specify the type of response you want
  • Combine Roles: Mix technical expertise with soft skills
  • Iterate: Refine roles based on the responses you get

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too Vague: "Act as an expert" - Expert in what?
  • Contradictory: "Be a detail-oriented big-picture thinker"
  • Overloaded: Adding too many roles confuses the AI
  • No Context: Forgetting to specify the task after the role
