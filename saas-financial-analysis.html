Risk Assessment & Overspend Alerts

⚠️

Google Vertex AI

Pay-per-use model creates budget uncertainty. Current $15K/month estimate could spike 3-5x with heavy ML workloads. Recommend strict usage monitoring and budget alerts.

⚡

Duplicate Functionality

Several tools have overlapping features (ChatGPT vs Perplexity for research, multiple video tools). Risk of paying for unused capabilities. Consider consolidation.

📈

Adoption Risk

High monthly per-user cost ($812) requires strong adoption to achieve ROI. Low usage rates could turn these investments into costly overhead.

💰

Enterprise Pricing Creep

Many tools show "custom pricing" for enterprise. Actual costs often 2-3x higher than listed prices. Budget should include 20% contingency.

Strategic Recommendations

  • Phase rollout: Start with high-ROI tools (ChatGPT, Notion, AdCreative.ai) to prove value before full deployment
  • Negotiate enterprise deals: Bundle purchases for 15-25% discounts, especially for Synthesia and Abnormal Security
  • Implement usage tracking: Deploy monitoring for all pay-per-use services with automatic alerts at 80% of budget
  • Create adoption programs: Invest 10% of tool budget in training to ensure full utilization and ROI achievement
  • Review quarterly: Audit actual usage vs. licenses to eliminate shelfware and optimize tier selection
  • Consider alternatives: Evaluate open-source options for ML workloads to reduce Vertex AI dependency
  • Establish tool governance: Create approval process for new tools to prevent shadow IT and redundancy
